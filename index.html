To set up visual studio...


- search up on google "visual studio download" - click on the first link you see - the site will show you different options: "Windows" ".Deb" ".rpm" "Mac" - click on the blue button that resenbels your laptop/desktop - once clicked, it will be downloaded and it will take you to your "downloads" - once you are on downloads, you will see the visual studio logo and name - the logo is a blue ribbon tied together horizontaly - click on the option - you will be taken to visual studio


If you want to code on visual studio platform...


- before you download the platform - create a folder - when done, left click on the "open" option - when clicked, it will take you to your downloads - click on desktop and then find the file you have created (tip- it will be in alphabetical order) - when you have chosen the file, on the left side, left click on the blankspace - out of the options, click on new file - name the file what ever you want and then...
